Steve Harrington

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steve_Harrington

Steve Harrington is a fictional character from the Netflix television show Stranger Things, portrayed by Joe Keery. While starting out as a typical unlikable jock, Steve has grown into a more protecting and caring character as the show has progressed, a development that has received widespread acclaim from critics and fans alike and has led to him becoming one of the show's most beloved and enduring characters.[1] Initially a part of the recurring cast, Keery was promoted to the main cast in the second season. Steve is a side antagonist turned protagonist at the end of season 1, and one of the main protagonists of season 2, season 3, and season 4.

Fictional Character biography

Season 1

Steve is initially portrayed as a stereotypical 1980s popular "jock" with an antagonistic personality, and is shown to be dating Nancy Wheeler. After he harasses Jonathan Byers by breaking his camera, Nancy seems to become close to Jonathan. This causes Steve to see the error of his ways and abandon his former friends. Traveling to the Byers' home to apologize, he becomes involved in Nancy and Jonathan's fight against the Demogorgon, which they successfully banish.

Season 2

Steve's relationship with Nancy is stressed, and he breaks up with her after she won't say she loves him, calling her out after her drunken tirade at a party. He also finds himself at odds with Billy Hargrove, a new student who seeks to become the school's tough guy. Steve becomes involved with Mike Wheeler and his friends after Dustin asks him to help find his "pet" D'Artagnan, unaware it is a creature from the Upside Down that his friends call a "Demodog."

Season 3

Steve started working in the ice cream shop Scoops Ahoy, where he met Robin Buckley, another Hawkins High School student who initially didn't like him. What started as a workplace rivalry evolved into one of the series' most celebrated platonic friendships after Robin came out to him.

Season 4

He helped the group when they planned to kill Vecna. He, along with Nancy, Robin, and Eddie, gets trapped in the Upside Down. On their way, he confesses to Nancy that he still loves her and thanks her for making him a better person. Despite their efforts, they fail to prevent Vecna's 4th gate from opening.
